ANGSD - Analysis of Next Generation Sequencing Data
====================================================

ANGSD performs population genetics analyses directly from BAM/CRAM files
without requiring hard-called genotypes. Ideal for low-coverage data.

## Installation
conda install -c bioconda angsd

## Key Analyses

### Allele Frequencies (from BAM files directly)
angsd -bam bam_list.txt -GL 1 -doMaf 1 -doMajorMinor 1 \
      -SNP_pval 1e-6 -minMapQ 20 -minQ 20 -nThreads 8 -out results

### Site Frequency Spectrum (SFS)
# Step 1: Estimate SFS
angsd -bam pop1_bams.txt -doSaf 1 -GL 1 -anc ancestral.fa -nThreads 8 -out pop1
realSFS pop1.saf.idx > pop1.sfs

# Step 2: 2D SFS for FST
angsd -bam pop1_bams.txt -doSaf 1 -GL 1 -anc ancestral.fa -out pop1
angsd -bam pop2_bams.txt -doSaf 1 -GL 1 -anc ancestral.fa -out pop2
realSFS pop1.saf.idx pop2.saf.idx > pop1_pop2.2dsfs

### FST Estimation
realSFS fst index pop1.saf.idx pop2.saf.idx -sfs pop1_pop2.2dsfs -fstout fst_results
realSFS fst stats fst_results.fst.idx                        # Global FST
realSFS fst stats2 fst_results.fst.idx -win 50000 -step 25000 -type 2  # Windowed FST

### Nucleotide Diversity (theta)
# Step 1: Get per-site thetas
angsd -bam bam_list.txt -doSaf 1 -GL 1 -anc ancestral.fa -out results
realSFS results.saf.idx > results.sfs
angsd -bam bam_list.txt -doThetas 1 -doSaf 1 -GL 1 -anc ancestral.fa -pest results.sfs -out results

# Step 2: Window-based statistics
thetaStat do_stat results.thetas.idx -win 50000 -step 25000 -outnames theta_windows
# Output includes: Watterson's theta, pairwise theta (pi), Tajima's D

### PCA (from genotype likelihoods)
angsd -bam bam_list.txt -GL 1 -doGlf 2 -doMajorMinor 1 -doMaf 1 \
      -SNP_pval 1e-6 -nThreads 8 -out pca_input
# Then use PCAngsd:
pcangsd -b pca_input.beagle.gz -o pca_output -t 8

### Heterozygosity
angsd -bam sample.bam -doSaf 1 -GL 1 -anc ancestral.fa -out sample
realSFS sample.saf.idx > sample.sfs
# Heterozygosity = sfs[1] / sum(sfs)

### Association Testing
angsd -bam bam_list.txt -GL 1 -doMaf 1 -doMajorMinor 1 -doAsso 1 \
      -yBin phenotype.txt -out assoc_results

## Key Parameters
-GL 1              Genotype likelihood model (1=SAMtools, 2=GATK)
-doMaf 1           Estimate allele frequencies
-doMajorMinor 1    Infer major/minor alleles
-SNP_pval 1e-6     P-value threshold for SNP calling
-minMapQ 20        Minimum mapping quality
-minQ 20           Minimum base quality
-nThreads 8        Number of threads
